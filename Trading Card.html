Tawny Mole Cricket

Little Sally Strawberry thought it was an ordinary day, until she felt the rumble from below...

  • Scientific Name: Neoscapteriscus vicinus
  • Element: Earth
  • Strife Specibus: Clawkind
  • Moves: Dig - Burrow under the ground.
    Slash - Cut using razor sharp claws.

From the distant lands of South America, these crickets have come to wreak havoc upon the plants of the Southeast.
